CyberToken Wallet

Use tokens, not passwords.

Take back complete ownership of your full online persona with a single App. Use verified tokens to proof that claims you make about yourself are true. Personally authorise every instance where these tokens are used on your behalf in online interactions.

Each token, contains within it, a digital fingerprint of the information about you that it represents. This is encoded in such a way that the information the token represents can't be extracted from the token itself, unless you also explicitly provide the information you5 want to share and specifically exactly how you authorise the information to be used and for how long.
